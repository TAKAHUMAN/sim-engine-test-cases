# Foretinib PK/PD Simulation - PRCC
## Test Case: Choueiri et al. (2012) with PK calibration to Eder et al. (2010)

### Overview
This test case implements a foretinib PK/PD workflow for the phase II papillary renal cell carcinoma study by Choueiri et al. (2012).

Model blocks:

- PK: 2-compartment oral absorption model with first-pass bioavailability and no time-dependent clearance.
- Dosing: Cohort A 240 mg once daily on days 1-5 every 14 days; Cohort B 80 mg once daily continuously.
- PD: indirect-response biomarker model for HGF, soluble MET, soluble VEGFR-2, and VEGF.
- Clinical outcomes: Monte Carlo PFS, OS, objective response, and safety incidence simulation.
- Validation: trial-level comparison against Choueiri phase II outcomes and PK exposure anchors from Eder et al. phase I data.

### References

Choueiri TK, Vaishampayan U, Rosenberg JE, et al. Phase II and biomarker study of the dual MET/VEGFR2 inhibitor foretinib in patients with papillary renal cell carcinoma. *J Clin Oncol.* 2013;31(2):181-186. doi: 10.1200/JCO.2012.43.3383.

Eder JP, Shapiro GI, Appleman LJ, et al. A phase I study of foretinib, a multi-targeted inhibitor of c-Met and vascular endothelial growth factor receptor 2. *Clin Cancer Res.* 2010;16(13):3507-3516. doi: 10.1158/1078-0432.CCR-10-0574.

### Source Split

- Clinical PD/outcome source: Choueiri et al. phase II PRCC study.
- PK calibration source: Eder et al. phase I foretinib study, using Cmax, Tmax, AUC0-24, and half-life anchors at the 240 mg daily exposure scale.
- The 80 mg daily PK targets are dose-proportional proxies derived from the 240 mg daily phase I exposure anchors.

### Files

- `foretinib_pkpd_model.py`: Full PK/PD simulation and validation workflow.
- `requirements.txt`: Minimal Python package requirements.
- `results/pk_calibration_report.txt`: Summary of PK literature validation and clinical outcome comparison.
- `results/validation_summary*.csv`: Trial endpoint validation tables.
- `results/pk_literature_validation.csv`: PK comparison against published/proxy exposure anchors.
- `results/table_pk_parameters.csv`: Current parameterization and sources.
- `results/*.csv`: Summary outputs and sensitivity checks from the current run.
- Long per-timepoint profile tables are regenerated by the script but ignored in git to keep the test case lightweight.

### How To Run
From repository root:

```bash
python test_cases/RCC/foretinib/choueiri_2012/foretinib_pkpd_model.py
```

This regenerates CSV outputs under `results/` and figures under `results/figures/`.

### Notes

- This is an exploratory simulation test case, not a clinical decision model.
- Published CL/F, Vd/F, and absolute bioavailability were not directly available in the open-access text used for this build.
- The day-1 phase I exposure scale is not fully reproduced by the fixed-parameter model; day-8 exposure and half-life are prioritized for steady-state PRCC regimen comparison.
